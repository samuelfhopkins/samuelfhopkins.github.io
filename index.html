I am an assistant professor in the mathematics department of Howard University in Washington, DC. My research is in algebraic and enumerative combinatorics.

Short academic bio: I joined the Howard math department in 2021. From 2018 to 2021 I was an NSF postdoc at the University of Minnesota school of mathematics, where my mentor was Vic Reiner. In 2018 I got my PhD from the MIT math department, where my advisor was Alex Postnikov. I did my undergraduate studies at Reed College.
