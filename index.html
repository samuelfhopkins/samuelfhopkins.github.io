I am an Assistant Professor in the mathematics department of Howard University. My research is in algebraic and enumerative combinatorics and related areas.

Short academic bio: I joined the Howard math department in August 2021. From September 2018 to May 2021 I was an NSF postdoc at the University of Minnesota school of mathematics, where my mentor was Vic Reiner. In June 2018 I got my PhD from the MIT math department, where my advisor was Alex Postnikov. I did my undergraduate studies at Reed College.
