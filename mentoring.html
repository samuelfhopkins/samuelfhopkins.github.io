REU at UMN

In the summer of 2019, I was a mentor at the University of Minnesota Combinatorics and Algebra REU. I mentored a group consisting of Quang Dao, Julian Wellman, Calvin Yost-Wolff, and Sylvester Zhang. Their work resulted in the paper Rowmotion Orbits of Trapezoid Posets, published in the Electronic Journal of Combinatorics.

RSI at MIT

In the summer of 2014, I was a mentor at the RSI program in the MIT math department. There I mentored two students: Petar Gaydarov and Ingrid Zhang. With Petar Gaydarov, we wrote the paper Parking functions and tree inversions revisited, published in Advances in Applied Mathematics. With Ingrid Zhang, we wrote the paper A note on statistical averages for oscillating tableaux, published in the Electronic Journal of Combinatorics.
